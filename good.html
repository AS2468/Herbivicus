Good Luck & Prosperity

Tulsi

A revered plant in the Indian subcontinent and considered as holy in the Hindu religion, there are several varieties available in the market. Manjari, Lakshmi Tulsi, Krishna Tulasi, Ram Tulasi, Kapoor Tulsi, Trittavu Tulsi are some that households in India have been growing and worshipping for ages.
Botanical name: Ocimum Sanctum
Benefits: brings peace, prosperiety and aethestic vibes

Orchids

The Orchidaceae are a diverse and widespread family of flowering plants, with blooms that are often colourful and fragrant, commonly known as the orchid family. They normally bloom 1-2 times a year and the blooms can last anywhere from 60-120 days.
Botanical name: Orchidaceae
Benefits: orchids have the unique ability to bring good luck and fortune in love.

Lucky Bamboo

It is ideal to place the plant in the east to focus on family or the south-east which is the nucleus of wealth. The highlight, of course, is that it is pretty low maintenance, making it the best choice for those who cannot exercise their green skills.
Botanical name: Dracaena sanderiana
Benefits: The Lucky Bamboo brings harmony within the five key Feng Shui elements- Fire, Earth, Wood, Water and Metal thereby harbouring and nurturing positive life experiences.

Morning glory

These pretty summer flowers open their prolific bell-shaped blooms in the early morning before the summer’s midday heat, so it’s not surprisingly they have earned their reputation for helping to brighten your day.
Botanical name: Ipomoea purpurea
Benefits: Morning glories in the garden are said to bring peace and happiness and its seeds under your pillow encourage restful sleep free of nightmares.

Money Plant

The money plant is a strong air purifier. It cleanses the air and the energy in the environment. It is advisable to place one near your TV, computer, and even the router as it can soak up the harmful radiations from electronic devices. Be sure to keep the money plant indoors and never place it outside.
Botanical name: Epipremnum aureum
Benefits: reduces anxiety and stress and makes it a calm and serene environment at home.

Jasmine

The sweet-smelling flowers of the jasmine plant uplift your spirits with the fragrance. It also attracts and channels positive energy, and can soothe your frayed nerves when you come home after a stressful day at work.
Botanical name: Jasminum
Benefits: Jasmine attracts love and money into your home, and encourages prophetic dreams.

Peony flowers

These lovely pink or red flowers represent the Divine Feminine or Shakti and should be placed in the southwest direction to attract true love and promote meaningful and fulfilling relationships.
Botanical name: Paeonia
Benefits: promotes feminine beauty and good reproductive health for women.

Lotus

Although you’d think this a slightly unusual choice for houseplants, the lotus symbolises the Goddess Lakshmi and even the Buddha. It is a very sacred plant and also has medicinal properties.
Botanical name: Nelumbo nucifera
Benefits: can bring you great peace and prosperity and even bring you closer to spiritual enlightenment.
